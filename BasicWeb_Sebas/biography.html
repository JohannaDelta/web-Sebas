Who is Sebastián Reátegui Delta?

Meet the artist

Sebastián is a Peruvian/Greek actor, with university studies and theatrical workshops in different countries and international experience in leading roles in movies, plays and commercials.



TRAINING/EDUCATION:
- International School of Dramactic corporeal Mime, France.
- University of Maryland, School of Theatre (B.A), USA.
- Actor's Movement, Spain.
- The Shakespeare Theatre Company, USA.
- Singer's Process Workshop, The Studio Theatre, USA.
- In the Biz, University of Maryland, USA.
- Advanced workshop for Actors, Peru.
- Musical Theatre Workshop (Preludio), Peru.
